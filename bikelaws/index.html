Cyclists must travel in the same direction as traffic. 2201.1

Cyclists must obey traffic control signals, including stoplights. 1201.15

Cyclists may ride between lanes. 1201.3(b)

Cyclists may ride on the sidewalk except in this area: Massachusetts Avenue to the north, Constitution Avenue to the south, 23rd Street NW, to the west and 2nd Street, NE to the east

Cyclists under 16 must wear a helmet. Title 50 Subtitle V Chapter 16

Drivers cannot stop, stand, or park in bike lanes. 2405.1(g) report

Drivers must only open their door when it is safe for pedestrians. 'Dooring' is the fault of the driver. 2214.4

Drivers must yield right of way to cyclists. Title 18, 1200.3 report

Cyclists may talk on cell phones while moving. 1731.04

Cyclists must have a bell. 1204.5
